Strategies for Managing Anxiety

Cognitive Strategies (SUDS 1-4)

  • 3/10 ‚Äì Growing Tension: Apply rational self-talk, use problem-solving techniques

Physical Interventions (SUDS 5-10)
  • 5/10 ‚Äì Body Activation: Start deep breathing exercises (4-7-8 breathing pattern)
  • 6/10 - Physical Tension: Progressive muscle relaxation, grounding exercises
  • 7/10 ‚Äì Strong Physical: Cold exposure (face/hands), vigorous exercise
  • 8/10 ‚Äì Intense Anxiety: Bilateral stimulation, rhythmic movement, cold shower
  • 9/10 ‚Äì Near Panic: Safe space retreat, emergency breathing kit, crisis contacts
  • 10/10 ‚Äì Panic Attack: Emergency response plan, medication if prescribed, immediate support person
Remember:
  • Below SUDS 5: Focus on thought patterns and cognitive restructuring
  • Above SUDS 5: Prioritize physical interventions to regulate nervous system
  • Always start with breathing regardless of level
  • Document what works best for you at each level
